Project Overview

This research prototype explores an explainable deep learning approach for predicting Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD). The system analyses both retinal fundus images and clinical biomarkers such as serum creatinine and albumin levels, combining visual and numerical insights to estimate CKD risk.

Why Retinal Images?

The retina contains microvascular structures that reflect systemic vascular conditions. Changes in these retinal blood vessels often correspond to impaired kidney function. This connection allows deep learning models to detect subtle abnormalities in retinal patterns that may indicate early CKD.
